## Slide 2: The Universal Problem

**The "Intent-Reality Gap" is the Silent Killer of Complex Systems.**

Whether you are building software, enforcing laws, or investigating corruption, there are always two versions of the world:

1. **The Intent (The Plan):** Documentation, Architecture Diagrams, Laws, Public Statements.
2. **The Reality (The Mess):** Code, Cloud Infrastructure, Emails, Financial Transactions.

**The Problem:** These two worlds are disconnected.

* **In Tech:** This is called **"Architectural Drift."** (Result: Downtime, Security Breaches).
* **In Media:** This is called **"Corruption."** (Result: Fraud, Hidden Conflicts of Interest).

*Current tools (Search/Vector DBs) only look at the text. They miss the **structure**.*

## Slide 3: The Solution

**Meet CodeGraft: The Active Governance Engine.**

CodeGraft is an AI-powered platform that ingests your scattered data, "grafts" it into a unified Knowledge Graph, and actively monitors for drift.

**The Core Loop:**

1. **Ingest:** Connects to "Intent" sources (Docs/PDFs) and "Reality" sources (Code/Logs).
2. **Reason (GraphRAG):** Uses Graph Retrieval-Augmented Generation to understand relationships, not just keywords.
3. **Act:** Automatically blocks drift or flags contradictions in real-time.
